Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 6, 2025

BurgTV ("we", "our", "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Data We Collect

BurgTV collects only the data strictly necessary to provide the service:

  • Device identifier: A randomly generated unique ID for each device. We do not collect MAC addresses or other hardware identifiers.
  • Account data: Email and password (encrypted) if you register for a Premium account.
  • Payment data: Processed securely by Stripe. We do not store credit card numbers on our servers.
  • Playlist URLs: M3U playlist URLs you add to the app (stored locally on device and optionally synced to our servers if you enable cloud backup).
  • Technical data: Device type, app version, operating system for technical support.

2. What We Do NOT Collect

  • Device MAC address
  • Content you watch
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, or other personal data

3. How We Use Data

  • Provide and maintain the BurgTV service
  • Manage your account and Premium license
  • Sync settings across devices
  • Provide technical support
  • Improve the application

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: For authentication and database (EU). Privacy Policy
  • Stripe: For payment processing. Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare Turnstile: For anti-bot protection (does not collect personal data). Privacy Policy

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You can request deletion at any time through account settings or by contacting us.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Export your data
  • Object to processing

7. Security

We use HTTPS encryption for all communications. Passwords are encrypted with secure algorithms. Data access is limited and monitored.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be communicated through the app.

9. Contact

Privacy questions: info@burgtv.com
